**Team: Flexible Highwayman Core**
Heroes:
- R4 Any non-Musketeer support or damage hero: `flexible support`, `flexible damage`, `flexible control`, `flexible sustain`
- R3 Healer, usually Vestal or Occultist: `healing`, `stun/control`, `damage`, `utility`
- R2 Highwayman: `Wicked Slice` or `Open Vein`, `Pistol Shot`, `Point Blank Shot`, `Duelist's Advance`
- R1 Any frontline hero, or another Highwayman: `frontline damage`, `reach`, `control`, `self-sustain`

Strategy:
- The video presents Highwayman as broadly plug-and-play: pick a healer, put Highwayman in rank 2, then fill the other two slots with almost anything useful.
- Open with `Duelist's Advance` from rank 2, then use `Point Blank Shot` after he moves forward.
- Prefer bleed loadout where enemies can bleed; swap to direct damage when they cannot.
