**Team: Flexible Plague Doctor Shells**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Houndmaster or Jester: `Hound's Rush`, `Hound's Harry`, `Cry Havoc` / `Harvest`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R2 Flagellant or Bounty Hunter: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Reclaim` / `Collect Bounty`, `Uppercut`, `Flashbang`
- R1 Occultist, Leper, or Hellion: `Sacrifical Stab`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Hands from the Abyss` / `Chop`, `Solemnity`, `Intimidate` / `Wicked Hack`, `Iron Swan`, `Barbaric YAWP!`

Strategy:
- The video's team section emphasizes that Plague Doctor fits into many strong shells rather than only one named composition.
- Her core job is opening control with `Blinding Gas`, backline damage with `Plague Grenade`, and cleanup/sustain through `Noxious Blast` and `Battlefield Medicine`.
- Pair her with heroes that exploit time bought by stuns: Flagellant for backline bleed, Houndmaster for reach/stress tools, or Occultist/Bounty Hunter for more stun density.
