**Team: Mark**
Heroes:
- R4 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Sniper's Mark`/`Call the Shot`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`
- R3 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`, `Blackjack`
- R2 Bounty Hunter: `Collect Bounty`, `Mark for Death`, `Uppercut`, `Flashbang`, `Finish Him`
- R1 Occultist: `Sacrifical Stab`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Vulnerability Hex`, `Hands from the Abyss`

Strategy:
- Use marks and protection debuffs to set up `Collect Bounty`, `Hound's Rush`, and `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot` kills.
- Occultist and Bounty Hunter provide stuns while the backline finishes priority targets.
- This is a strong burst team, but it needs enough speed/control to avoid losing tempo to mark setup.

**Team: Leper Hook**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Jester: `Harvest`, `Slice Off`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R2 Bounty Hunter: `Collect Bounty`, `Come Hither`, `Uppercut`, `Flashbang`, `Finish Him`
- R1 Leper: `Chop`, `Hew`, `Solemnity`, `Intimidate`

Strategy:
- Bounty Hunter pulls enemies forward with `Come Hither` so Leper can delete them.
- Jester buffs Leper and Bounty Hunter accuracy/speed while Plague Doctor controls the backline.
- Use stuns and pulls to decide which enemy gets exposed to the frontline damage first.

**Team: Backline Cleave**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Hound's Harry`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Blackjack`
- R2 Bounty Hunter: `Collect Bounty`, `Come Hither`, `Uppercut`, `Flashbang`, `Finish Him`
- R1 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`

Strategy:
- `Plague Grenade`, `Hound's Harry`, and `Rain of Sorrows` pressure enemy back ranks together.
- Bounty Hunter controls enemy formation with pulls/stuns and finishes weakened targets.
- The team is especially strong in normal dungeons where control and damage-over-time can snowball quickly.
