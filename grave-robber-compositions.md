**Team: Lunge/Sniper**
Heroes:
- R4 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`, `Rallying Flare`/`Skeet Shot`
- R3 Grave Robber: `Lunge`, `Shadow Fade`, `Thrown Dagger`, `Poison Dart`
- R2 Highwayman: `Wicked Slice`, `Pistol Shot`, `Point Blank Shot`, `Duelist's Advance`, `Open Vein`
- R1 Crusader or flexible frontliner: `Smite`, `Stunning Blow`, `Battle Heal`, `Holy Lance`, `Inspiring Cry`

Strategy:
- Grave Robber opens with `Lunge`, then uses `Shadow Fade` or party movement to reset.
- Highwayman and Crusader/frontliner tolerate the shuffle pattern and keep damage flowing.
- Arbalest/Musketeer gives backline reach and healing while Grave Robber deletes priority targets.

**Team: Pick to the Face**
Heroes:
- R4 Vestal: `Judgement`, `Dazzling Light`, `Divine Grace`, `Divine Comfort`
- R3 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R2 Grave Robber: `Pick to the Face`, `Shadow Fade`, `Thrown Dagger`, `Poison Dart`
- R1 Man-at-Arms: `Crush`, `Rampart`, `Bellow`, `Defender`, `Retribution`

Strategy:
- Grave Robber plays from the front/mid ranks and uses `Pick to the Face` for armor-piercing damage.
- Plague Doctor and Man-at-Arms provide control so Grave Robber can safely focus priority targets.
- Vestal covers sustain in a slower, more stable setup than the lunge team.

**Team: Flashing Daggers**
Heroes:
- R4 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Hound's Harry`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`
- R3 Grave Robber: `Flashing Daggers`, `Thrown Dagger`, `Poison Dart`, `Toxin Trickery`
- R2 Jester: `Dirk Stab`, `Harvest`, `Slice Off`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R1 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`

Strategy:
- Stack cleave and damage-over-time into ranks 2-3 with `Flashing Daggers`, `Harvest`, `Hound's Harry`, and Flagellant bleeds.
- Jester improves speed/accuracy so the cleave engine lands reliably.
- Houndmaster and Flagellant add sustain options while Grave Robber contributes ranged finishing damage.
