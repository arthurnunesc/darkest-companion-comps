**Team: Standard Chop**
Heroes:
- R4 Vestal: `Judgement`, `Dazzling Light`, `Divine Grace`, `Divine Comfort`
- R3 Jester: `Finale`, `Slice Off`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R2 Leper: `Chop`, `Hew`, `Solemnity`, `Intimidate`
- R1 Shieldbreaker: `Pierce`, `Puncture`, `Adder's Kiss`, `Serpent Sway`

Strategy:
- Jester uses `Battle Ballad` to patch Leper's accuracy/speed while Vestal keeps the party stable.
- Leper stays in the main damage slot and uses `Chop`/`Hew`, with `Solemnity` and `Intimidate` covering sustain and mitigation.
- Shieldbreaker gives the team mobile armor-piercing reach so Leper is not the only answer to protected or awkwardly positioned targets.

**Team: Damage Control**
Heroes:
- R4 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Hound's Harry`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`
- R3 Jester: `Harvest`, `Finale`, `Slice Off`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R2 Occultist: `Weakening Curse`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Vulnerability Hex`, `Hands from the Abyss`
- R1 Leper: `Chop`, `Hew`, `Solemnity`, `Intimidate`

Strategy:
- Stack `Intimidate` and `Weakening Curse` to heavily reduce incoming damage while Jester improves speed/accuracy.
- Occultist provides stun, debuff, mark, and emergency healing support from the front/mid ranks.
- Houndmaster adds reach, bleed/mark utility, stress control, and optional guard support so the team can survive longer fights without relying only on raw healing.

**Team: Rot Squad**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Hound's Harry`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`
- R2 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`
- R1 Leper: `Chop`, `Hew`, `Solemnity`, `Intimidate`

Strategy:
- Use Plague Doctor and Flagellant to rot down the backline with blight and bleed while Leper anchors the front.
- Houndmaster contributes reach, bleed cleave, stress recovery, and defensive utility.
- Leper can spend turns on `Intimidate` or `Solemnity` while damage-over-time effects finish enemies, then switch back to `Chop` when priority targets are exposed.
