**Team: Plug and Play**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Jester: `Harvest`, `Slice Off`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R2 Abomination: `Transform`, `Manacles`, `Beast's Bile`, `Absolution`, `Rake`, `Rage`, `Slam`
- R1 Hellion or flexible frontliner: `Wicked Hack`, `Iron Swan`, `Barbaric YAWP!`, `If It Bleeds`, `Bleed Out`

Strategy:
- The video presents this as the easier, lower-level team; the frontliner is flexible, with Hellion used as the example.
- Jester's `Battle Ballad` and `Inspiring Tune` offset Abomination's stress and improve tempo.
- Plague Doctor controls and blights while Abomination stuns in human form or transforms when damage is needed.

**Team: Mid/Late Transform Team**
Heroes:
- R4 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`, `Lick Wounds`
- R3 Abomination: `Transform`, `Manacles`, `Beast's Bile`, `Absolution`, `Rake`, `Rage`, `Slam`
- R2 Bounty Hunter: `Collect Bounty`, `Mark for Death` or `Finish Him`, `Uppercut`, `Flashbang`
- R1 Occultist: `Sacrifical Stab`, `Weakening Curse`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Vulnerability Hex`, `Hands from the Abyss`

Strategy:
- This version assumes Abomination transforms more often, so Houndmaster's `Cry Havoc` helps manage party stress.
- Occultist and Bounty Hunter provide stun/mark control while Abomination supplies burst and disruption.
- Houndmaster is flexible: keep `Hound's Rush` for rank 4 reach, `Cry Havoc` for stress, and swap the middle utilities by dungeon.
