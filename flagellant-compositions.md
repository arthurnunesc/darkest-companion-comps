**Team: Standard Bleed**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Hound's Harry`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`, `Blackjack`
- R2 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`
- R1 Hellion: `Wicked Hack`, `Iron Swan`, `Barbaric YAWP!`, `If It Bleeds`, `Bleed Out`

Strategy:
- Stack bleed with Flagellant, Houndmaster, and Hellion while Plague Doctor controls the backline.
- `Rain of Sorrows` and `Plague Grenade` pressure ranks 3-4; Hellion deletes priority targets with reach.
- Multiple control and sustain tools keep damage intake manageable.

**Team: Mark**
Heroes:
- R4 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Sniper's Mark`/`Call the Shot`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`
- R3 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`, `Blackjack`
- R2 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`
- R1 Bounty Hunter: `Collect Bounty`, `Mark for Death`, `Uppercut`, `Flashbang`, `Finish Him`

Strategy:
- Mark targets with Arbalest/Musketeer, Houndmaster, or Bounty Hunter, then burst them down.
- Flagellant provides bleed pressure and emergency healing without consuming the team's main damage turns.
- Bounty Hunter adds stuns, pulls, and execution damage into marked or stunned enemies.

**Team: Sustain**
Heroes:
- R4 Vestal: `Judgement`, `Dazzling Light`, `Divine Grace`, `Divine Comfort`
- R3 Jester: `Dirk Stab`, `Harvest`, `Slice Off`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R2 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`, `Redeem`
- R1 Leper: `Chop`, `Hew`, `Solemnity`, `Intimidate`

Strategy:
- Lean on Vestal, Jester, Flagellant, and Leper self-healing to outlast long fights.
- Jester accelerates the team with `Battle Ballad` and fixes stress while the front line grinds enemies down.
- Flagellant can heal others while staying dangerous at low HP.

**Team: Martyr**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Jester: `Dirk Stab`, `Harvest`, `Slice Off`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R2 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`, `Redeem`, `Endure`
- R1 Man-at-Arms: `Crush`, `Rampart`, `Bellow`, `Defender`, `Retribution`

Strategy:
- Build around keeping Flagellant active under pressure, then using his low-health power and recovery tools.
- Man-at-Arms protects, ripostes, and controls speed/accuracy while Plague Doctor stuns.
- Jester keeps stress under control so Flagellant's risky play pattern remains sustainable.
