**Team: Standard Blight Team**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Vestal: `Judgement`, `Dazzling Light`, `Divine Grace`, `Divine Comfort`
- R2 Shieldbreaker: `Pierce`, `Puncture`, `Adder's Kiss`, `Impale`, `Expose`, `Serpent Sway`
- R1 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`

Strategy:
- Plague Doctor and Shieldbreaker stack blight while Flagellant supplies bleed pressure and emergency sustain.
- `Blinding Gas` and `Puncture` control dangerous backliners while Shieldbreaker pierces protection.
- Vestal keeps the party stable so the damage-over-time engine can finish fights safely.

**Team: Double Impale**
Heroes:
- R4 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R3 Shieldbreaker: `Pierce`, `Puncture`, `Adder's Kiss`, `Impale`, `Expose`, `Serpent Sway`
- R2 Shieldbreaker: `Pierce`, `Puncture`, `Adder's Kiss`, `Impale`, `Expose`, `Serpent Sway`
- R1 Vestal or flexible healer: `Judgement`, `Dazzling Light`, `Divine Grace`, `Divine Comfort`

Strategy:
- Use repeated `Impale` to apply party-wide enemy pressure and stack blight quickly.
- Double Shieldbreaker gives strong armor-piercing reach and self-protection through `Serpent Sway`.
- The healer stabilizes while Plague Doctor adds stun/blight control.

**Team: Mark**
Heroes:
- R4 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Sniper's Mark`/`Call the Shot`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`
- R3 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`, `Blackjack`
- R2 Shieldbreaker: `Pierce`, `Puncture`, `Adder's Kiss`, `Expose`, `Serpent Sway`
- R1 Occultist: `Sacrifical Stab`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Vulnerability Hex`, `Hands from the Abyss`

Strategy:
- Occultist, Houndmaster, and Arbalest/Musketeer mark or debuff targets for burst damage.
- Shieldbreaker covers protection-heavy enemies and can pull/disrupt with `Puncture`.
- The team has reach into every enemy rank and enough control to stop priority threats before they act repeatedly.
