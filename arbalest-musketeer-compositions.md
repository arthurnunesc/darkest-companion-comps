**Team: Marked For Death**
Heroes:
- R4 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Sniper's Mark`/`Call the Shot`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`
- R3 Houndmaster: `Hound's Rush`, `Target Whistle`, `Cry Havoc`, `Guard Dog`, `Blackjack`
- R2 Bounty Hunter: `Collect Bounty`, `Mark for Death`, `Uppercut`, `Flashbang`, `Finish Him`
- R1 Occultist: `Sacrifical Stab`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Vulnerability Hex`, `Hands from the Abyss`

Strategy:
- Stack marks and protection debuffs, then burst priority targets with Arbalest/Musketeer, Houndmaster, and Bounty Hunter.
- Occultist provides healing, marking, and frontline stun control.
- The team is strongest when it acts quickly enough to remove dangerous enemies before the mark setup becomes too slow.

**Team: Hybrid**
Heroes:
- R4 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`, `Rallying Flare`/`Skeet Shot`
- R3 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R2 Flagellant: `Punish`, `Rain of Sorrows`, `Exsanguinate`, `Reclaim`
- R1 Occultist: `Sacrifical Stab`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Vulnerability Hex`, `Hands from the Abyss`

Strategy:
- Mix backline blight/bleed pressure with Arbalest/Musketeer healing and utility.
- Plague Doctor and Occultist control dangerous targets while Flagellant supplies damage-over-time and sustain.
- Arbalest/Musketeer can shoot early, then shift into healing and support as the fight stabilizes.

**Team: Supreme Suppressors**
Heroes:
- R4 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`, `Rallying Flare`/`Skeet Shot`
- R3 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`, `Rallying Flare`/`Skeet Shot`
- R2 Man-at-Arms: `Crush`, `Rampart`, `Bellow`, `Defender`, `Retribution`
- R1 Occultist: `Sacrifical Stab`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Vulnerability Hex`, `Hands from the Abyss`

Strategy:
- Stack `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen` with `Bellow` to heavily reduce enemy accuracy and speed.
- Occultist and Man-at-Arms provide frontline control while the backline pair covers damage and healing.
- The team wins by making enemy turns unreliable, then cleaning up with ranged attacks and stuns.
