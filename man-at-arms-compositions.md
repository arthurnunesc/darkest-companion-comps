**Team: Support**
Heroes:
- R4 Vestal: `Judgement`, `Dazzling Light`, `Divine Grace`, `Divine Comfort`
- R3 Jester: `Harvest`, `Slice Off`, `Battle Ballad`, `Inspiring Tune`
- R2 Man-at-Arms: `Crush`, `Rampart`, `Bellow`, `Defender`, `Command`, `Bolster`
- R1 Leper: `Chop`, `Hew`, `Solemnity`, `Intimidate`

Strategy:
- Man-at-Arms protects and buffs while Jester fixes speed, accuracy, and stress.
- Leper becomes much more reliable with `Command`/`Battle Ballad` support.
- Vestal provides stable healing so the team can take slower, safer fights.

**Team: Rank 4**
Heroes:
- R4 Man-at-Arms: `Rampart`, `Bellow`, `Defender`, `Command`, `Bolster`
- R3 Plague Doctor: `Noxious Blast`, `Plague Grenade`, `Blinding Gas`, `Battlefield Medicine`
- R2 Bounty Hunter: `Collect Bounty`, `Mark for Death`, `Uppercut`, `Flashbang`, `Finish Him`
- R1 Occultist: `Sacrifical Stab`, `Wyrd Reconstruction`, `Vulnerability Hex`, `Hands from the Abyss`

Strategy:
- Man-at-Arms can operate from rank 4 as a support/control hero with `Bellow`, `Defender`, and buffs.
- Plague Doctor and Occultist stack stuns while Bounty Hunter executes controlled targets.
- The team uses control density rather than raw healing to reduce incoming damage.

**Team: The Wall**
Heroes:
- R4 Arbalest or Musketeer: `Sniper Shot`/`Aimed Shot`, `Suppressing Fire`/`Smokescreen`, `Battlefield Bandage`/`Patch Up`, `Rallying Flare`/`Skeet Shot`
- R3 Man-at-Arms: `Crush`, `Rampart`, `Bellow`, `Defender`, `Retribution`, `Bolster`
- R2 Man-at-Arms: `Crush`, `Rampart`, `Bellow`, `Defender`, `Retribution`, `Bolster`
- R1 Leper: `Chop`, `Hew`, `Solemnity`, `Intimidate`

Strategy:
- Double Man-at-Arms layers guard, riposte, `Bellow`, and defensive buffs.
- Leper provides heavy damage and self-sustain while the backliner patches healing and utility.
- The team is slow but extremely hard to break once defensive layers are established.
